Cpe Riverview Daycare

We offer full time daycare services in a warm and caring setting. We provide 2 snacks and lunch that is catered in a nut and pork free environment. We bring the children outside each day for physical activity and have an adjoining park. Children are offered a variety of activities throughout the day based on their interests and also have specified times of group time, arts & crafts, as well as many other learning experiences. We welcome you into our daycare and look forward to providing your child with a fun and safe learning environment.
